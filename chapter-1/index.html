Stuffed Grape Leaves (Warak Enab / Dolma)

A Mediterranean and Middle Eastern delicacy featuring tender grape leaves stuffed with a flavorful mixture of rice, herbs, and spices. Often served as a cold appetizer (Yalangi) or a warm main dish, it offers a perfect balance of savory and tangy flavors.

Ingredients

  • 500g Grape leaves (jarred in brine or fresh/blanched)
  • 2 cups Egyptian or short-grain rice, rinsed and soaked
  • 1 bunch of fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Pomegranate molasses
  • 1/2 cup Extra virgi
